Donate Goods and Services to Support TCI

In-kind donations are a valuable way to support the Thembi Community Initiative. We accept a variety of goods and services that can directly benefit our programs and the people we serve.

Items We Accept

  • School Supplies: Books, stationery, backpacks, and other educational materials.
  • Medical Supplies: First-aid kits, over-the-counter medications, and medical equipment (please contact us for specific needs).
  • Clothing: New or gently used clothing for children and adults.
  • Food: Non-perishable food items to support our nutrition programs.
  • Technology: Computers, tablets, and other devices to support our education and skills training programs.
  • Services: Professional services such as medical care, tutoring, or business consulting.

How to Donate In-Kind

To donate goods or services, please contact us to arrange a drop-off or pickup. We will provide you with a donation receipt for tax purposes.
